Last year together we brought One Billion Rising for Justice to
communities and events worldwide! This year’s theme, ONE BILLION
RISING REVOLUTION
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=siBDGEVLiQJPrGBBQQ6qkQ

, is a call to focus on revolution and change as an escalation of our
demand to end violence against women and girls once and for all.

ONE BILLION RISING REVOLUTION is an energy, a platform, a global
movement, a catalyst, a worldwide decision to end violence against
women, a demand for justice, a paradigm shift, an invitation, a
gathering of the ready, housed everywhere, housed in our hearts, you,
us, REVOLUTION.

With the tagline “Drum, Dance & Rise,” Risings will focus on
highlighting, creating and envisioning new, brave and radical artistic
initiatives to bring in the new revolutionary world of equality,
dignity and freedom for all women and girls.

V-season is now open for registration!

The 2015 V-Season will be a transitional year, during which the
structure of the V-Day campaigns will begin to shift to an even more
autonomous, activist based model.

2015 productions of “The Vagina Monologues” will be held during the
month of February, and if you wish to perform the play in other
months, you may purchase the rights from Dramatists Play Service.
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=hhfiVTDyP2x-Z9JNd9rrvQ

By focusing the benefit performances during one month - February - we
maximize the impact. Beginning in 2016, “The Vagina Monologues” will
be available free of charge to registered activists, every year solely
in the month of February. These productions will continue to raise
money for anti-violence work, and support and guidance will come from
a strong network of fellow activists. In 2016 and beyond, those
wishing to present “The Vagina Monologues” outside of February will
purchase the rights through DPS.
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=xiEE_MUZoMXMWYzQe7IUJQ

place any time during the year.
