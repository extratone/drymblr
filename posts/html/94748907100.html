Last week, the US Court of Appeals for the 5th Circuit blocked – for now – the closing of Mississippi’s last remaining clinic, the Jackson Women’s Health Organization.

*We need your help to provide new security measures for the last remaining abortion clinic in Mississippi*. The day before the federal appeals court’s decision, in another courtroom, our Clinic Defense Team was present when a Jackson city court judge found three Operation Rescue/Operation Save America protestors GUILTY of obstructing the sidewalk in front of and interfering with the clinic.

Our team worked for nearly a year to see these extremists brought to justice. But threats to the safety of the Jackson clinic staff and physicians remain.

Now that the courts have acted, we must redouble our efforts to ensure the safety of all patients and personnel.
