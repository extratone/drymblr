Dear Dry,

The 42nd anniversary of “Roe v. Wade” is just a few days away. To honor “Roe” we need to *take a stand for women’s health now [ http://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/dia/action3/common/public/?action_KEY=16989 ]*.

The US House of Representatives plans to vote on a 20-week extremist abortion ban this Thursday, January 22 – the anniversary of “Roe v. Wade”. This extremist bill, one that would ban abortion pre-viability, is a direct attack on “Roe”. A similar ban in Arizona was deemed unconstitutional by a federal appeals court, and the Supreme Court refused to review that decision – letting stand the decision that the 20-week ban is unconstitutional. Congress must stop wasting time and money on unconstitutional measures.
