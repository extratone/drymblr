Dear Dry ,

Watch FFL President Serrin Foster’s heartfelt response to the women and children
who have died from abortion–in contrast to the heartless response by a Planned
Parenthood representative to babies born alive and left to die, and the unrepentant
Kermit Gosnell charged with murdering nine babies forced to be born, then killed
after birth. Serrin also points out that abortion solves nothing; instead, the
woman goes home to the same situation that drove her to abortion in the first place.
Furthermore, she explains that abortion is an act of violence and discrimination.
“Abortion is a betrayal of feminism.”

Serrin was invited by Gov. Mike Huckabee to appear on the Huckabee Show which aired
numerous times last weekend. It is the number one weekend show on FOX News.

Serrin points out that New York City has the highest rate of abortion of any city.
New York State has the highest rate of abortion in any state. The United States
of America has the highest rate of abortion of a developed country. “When abortion
comes in first, women come in last–and our babies come in dead last.”
