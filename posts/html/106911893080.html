Happy New Year! And to all who contributed to start 2015 off on solid footing, I
thank you for your strategic sacrifice and support.

In preparation for events across the country commemorating the 42nd anniversary
of Roe v. Wade and Doe v. Bolton, FFL has materials for you to distribute at your
women’s centers, houses of worship, information booths, rallies, and more.

Please order bumper stickers, brochures, posters and placards thru our website
ASAP so you have them in time for your event. While the anniversary is on
January 22, many events are being held a week early!
FFL will have our placards available at One Life LA (new!), the Walk for Life
West Coast (and meet Board member Pat O'Kane at our booth!), March for Life Chicago
and March for Life in Washington DC.

Vice President Sally Winn will speak at the Students for Life Conferences in Maryland
and San Francisco, CA. Be sure to stop by our booth!

Please make plans now to attend these peaceful events and others across the country.
If you are coming to DC and wish to walk with us, we are organizing now and really
need helpers! Please contact info@feministsforlife.org [mailto:info@feministsforlife.org]
March forth!

Because women deserve better,

Serrin M. Foster

President
