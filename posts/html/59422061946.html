Dear Dry ,

I am so pleased to announce FFL’s BRAND NEW WEBSITE–just in time for the new school
year.

Visitors to our website have come to depend on FFL for our unique contributions
to the movement, and will now find it even easier to find needed resources–most
importantly:


* Pro-life feminist history first revealed by Feminists for Life historians
* Pioneering work on college campuses to advocate for resources and support
for pregnant and parenting students, to share pro-life feminist history, and
to provide meaningful support for student activists
* A library of resources and support for:

* pregnant women
* expectant fathers
* parents
* birthparents
* campus solutions
* workplace solutions
* Trailblazing work advocating for the protection of, and services for, women
who are victims and survivors of violence
* Pro-Woman Answers to Pro-Life QuestionsTM
