Next week marks the 41st anniversary of the Supreme Court’s Roe v. Wade and Doe
v. Bolton decisions. FFL President Serrin Foster and Vice President Sally Winn will
be participating in several events across the country, and FFL Chair of the Board
Patrick A. O'Kane will also be in San Francisco. Please join us!

Life inside the beautiful House Chambers of the Vermont State House in Montpelier.

Life of America. Refreshments to follow. There is no charge.
