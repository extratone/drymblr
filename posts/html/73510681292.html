 Our distance learning offerings are flexible and convenient, and were designed  for the working professional or busy student who cannot attend classes on our  NYC campus. NYSID instructors teach every course and the curriculum and  learning goals are the same as the on-site courses, but reformatted to  encourage participation and connection. Each course is asynchronous, meaning  that you do not have to “log in” at a set time. Instead, students watch recorded  lectures and demonstrations, participate in discussions, access handouts and  assignments, and communicate with the instructor and fellow students.

 Watch a brief video on our Introduction to Interior Design online course.
 About NYSID
 New York School of Interior Design is an accredited, not-for-profit college  devoted exclusively to interior design education and related disciplines. NYSID  offers certificate, undergraduate, and graduate programs in the fields of interior  design, design history and theory, sustainable design, interior lighting design,  and healthcare interior design. A wide range of classes are also offered through  our Institute for Continuing and Professional Studies.
