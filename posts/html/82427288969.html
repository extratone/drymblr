V-Leadership in Kabul, Afghanistan!

V-Day has committed to supporting PWCE (Promoting Women Capabilities
by Education) to run an extraordinary leadership center in the heart
of Kabul, Afghanistan. The center is providing free, non-formal
education in literacy, science, computers, family planning and English
for Afghan women and girls, as well as legal, psychosocial and
counseling support. It is currently serving 150 and has the capacity
to serve 250.
