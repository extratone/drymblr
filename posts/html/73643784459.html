What is Justice for the Women of Songo Mboyo? By Jessica Neuwirth

stationed near Songo Mboyo, a small remote village on the Congo river.
In this post, V-Day activist Jessica Neuwirth shares the details of
this event, and the justice, or lack thereof, that has resulted from
