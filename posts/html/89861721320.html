College-age women represent 44% of all women who have had abortions.
They cite the lack of resources & support as the overwhelming reasons why.

Feminists for Life, the leading advocate for pro-woman, pro-parent resources on campus, invites student activists
to come hear from women who know firsthand the lack of choice on campus. Come and be a part
of the revolution to give women the resources and choices they are missing!

Please join the creator of the Women Deserve Better than Abortion® Campaign
FFL President Serrin M. Foster,
rape survivor and student parent Joyce McCauley-Benner,
birthmother and former FFL board member Jessica O&;Connor-Petts,
and Johanna E. Young, a student mother of three with an abortion experience.

REVOLUTION ON CAMPUS
Thursday, July 10th at 2:00 PM
Rayburn Building Gold Room 2168
Capitol Hill - Washington, DC
Refreshments will be served.

We look forward to working with you and providing you with the latest tools, so you can be part of our revolution on campus!
Seating is limited for this intern briefing. RSVP now to intern@femininistsforlife.org.
