Next Week, We RISE for JUSTICE!

Top.jpeg

In one week, ONE BILLION women and men in 184 countries (and
counting!) will come together as one global movement led by local
activists addressing the themes of justice through art, indomitable
presence, direct demands and global solidarity as we RISE and bring
about revolutionary justice.

For those who ROSE last year, you know how inspiring, hectic and life
changing these next days will be.

Some things to keep in mind so that we can show the world WHAT ONE
BILLION RISING FOR JUSTICE LOOKS LIKE - please:

DOCUMENT YOUR RISINGS. Photos, videos, artwork, poems, press -
all of these things will help us tell the larger story and impact of
One Billion Rising for Justice in the coming months.

Remember to SHARE your RISING with the global movement at large so
that the world can see what you are doing to end violence against
women!
