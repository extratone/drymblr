Governor Mike Huckabee will interview FFL President Serrin Foster about the unmet
needs of women that drive them to abortion.
“This is what I love about Feminists for Life. We talk to everyone on the left,
right, independents and the politically homeless; those who have had an abortion,
and those who are pro-life. How else can we advance the needs of women and children?”
Serrin said.
Governor Huckabee, known for his strong pro-life record, supported the inclusion
of State Child Health Insurance for working pregnant women who had no health insurance
in Arkansas, a measure first advocated by FFL at the national level.
