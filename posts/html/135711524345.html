Dear V-Friends & Risers,

2015 was a revolutionary, evolutionary year for V-Day and One Billion
Rising. Your solidarity and support made it possible!
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=lVksUNteccZX6rzuGmYaLg

Activists worldwide called for system change towards ending violence
against women and girls. They staged plays, risings and events, rose
in the streets, changing laws, consciousness and culture with an
impact that is breathtaking and powerful.

It is our honor to share the 2015 Annual Report. These pages are a
snapshot of those global activities and an invitation to join us in
2016.

CLICK HERE for the 2015 Annual Report >
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=O7sk96XI6LwsHY_AF6RaeQ

This year, our V-Day campaigns expanded the dialogue around sexual
violence as activists raised funds and awareness through performances
of The Vagina Monologues and A Memory, A Monologue, A Rant and A
Prayer from a prison in upstate New York to a theater in Tehran. The
plays catalyzed important conversations and inspired new works of art.

City of Joy and V-World Farm continue to blossom and grow in the
Democratic Republic of Congo, transforming pain to power amidst
ongoing conflict in the region. The farm is going even deeper,
transforming pain into planting, healing the body and the earth.

In its third year, One Billion Rising called for Revolution, to
escalate tectonic global change. It was stunning to see how the call
resonated around the world. Never have we seen such diversity -
children, men, older people, LGTBQI, religious leaders, people of all
abilities, farmers, workers, NGOs, presidents and prime ministers.
Never have we seen such creativity; the movement continues to been led
by art, theater, music, dancing, poetry, drumming because that is
where revolution is activated.
