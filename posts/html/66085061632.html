Help Us End Campus Sexual Violence [ http://feministcampus.org/campus-sexual-assault-needs-to-end-announcing-our-newest-campaign/ ]

We know the numbers all-too-well, and we’re tired of seeing no progress on the rape and sexual assault epidemic that’s affecting campuses across the country. Our newest toolkit [ http://feministcampus.org/campus-sexual-assault-needs-to-end-announcing-our-newest-campaign/ ] is a great resource for campus activists looking to take action around ending sexual violence, and to celebrate its launch we’re showcasing stories from activists across the nation who successfully engaged in this work. Between how-tos on mandatory prevention education programs [ http://feministcampus.org/activism-101-how-i-mandated-sexual-assault-prevention-education-at-the-university-of-maryland/ ], breaking down rape culture [ http://feministcampus.org/rape-culture-is-prevalent-on-your-campus-and-on-every-campus/ ] and marching across campus [ http://feministcampus.org/roundtable-we-marched-against-sexual-violence-on-campus/ ], we’ve got a ton of resources for folks embarking on a mission to end rape and sexual assault at their college or university. (And stay posted, because they’re just gonna keep coming!) Don’t wait another minute - take action today [ http://feministcampus.org/campus-sexual-assault-needs-to-end-announcing-our-newest-campaign/ ]!

Don’t Forget to Vote! [ http://feministcampus.org/im-a-registered-virginia-feminist-celebrating-national-voter-registration-day-by-taking-action/ ]

As you’re aware, threats to abortion access and family planning aren’t just coming from extremists. There are major threats at the state and city levels. If you’re in VA, there’s a very important gubernatorial election TODAY [ http://feministcampus.org/im-a-registered-virginia-feminist-celebrating-national-voter-registration-day-by-taking-action/ ]. In cities like Albuquerque, there are very serious anti-choice ballot initiatives that are dependent on whether or not we show up to the polls. Please show up to the polls to demand respect and the right to choose!

If you’re interested in phone-banking at our LA or DC offices, email us! We’re at campusteam@feminist.org.
