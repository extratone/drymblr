In Less Than Five Weeks the World will RISE!

We are less than five weeks away from ONE BILLION RISING on 14
February 2013!

As we dance upon the path to ONE BILLION RISING, our email boxes are
full of amazing stories, beautiful photos and messages of solidarity
to RISE. Facebook is on fire with reasons to rise, and twitter is
abuzz with thousands of tweets from South Africa to India.

If you have not signed up to start a RISING in your community, or have
not joined an existing RISING, then please do so today!

Here’s a sampling of ONE BILLION RISING activities happening around
the world now and on 14 February:

* Activist Janelle Chung is currently cycling around Taiwan to get
people dancing for One Billion Rising and to raise money for
Garden of Hope women’s shelters, call centers, counseling
programs & advocacy campaigns in Taiwan, the U.S., Cambodia, and
South Africa!

* ONE BILLION RISING Amsterdam activists
tailored the One Billion Rising short film (http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=p_st5-nt7qH2apZcK-Yvew )
to make a Netherlands TV spot! Check it out here >
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=1bVKGvsW622JSciMXggm2g

* Singer Maya Azucena released a “behind the scenes” video for her
new ONE BILLION RISING song and music video set to be released
next week! Take a sneak peak here >
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=Nk_7qC-JbMac1cZ6kE8FoQ

* On 14 February, Australian Aboriginal Women in Urban Society
will be hosting an event titled “Rites of Passage to a Violent
Free Life.” The Event will be held at National Centre for
Indigenous Excellence, Redfern Sydney.

* The European Women’s Lobby is rising on 14 February with its
European and Belgian members and will be holding a Flashmob in
the heart of the City center of Bruxelles!
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=ta_8YbWnGDba7UoxllqfxA

* 20,000 women in Norwich, UK (roughly 1 in 3 of the female
population) will be conducting a mass dance to BREAK THE CHAIN
