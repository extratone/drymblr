Our team just returned from Orlando/Kissimmee, Florida area where clinics were targeted. We are working to keep targeted clinics open and safe in Wichita, KS, Albuquerque, NM, Nebraska, Texas, Maryland and upstate New York. Right now we are preparing to help clinics in Louisiana and Mississippi.


We fear that the Supreme Court’s ruling will embolden extremists. Anti-abortion extremists’ websites are crowing about their “huge victory” and predicting that remaining buffer zone laws across the country will be wiped out. *We can’t let that happen.* [ https://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=11227 ]


Our work to defend abortion clinics has become even more difficult. *That’s why we are asking for your support. In June, our team traveled to New Orleans and Baton Rouge to conduct clinic security assessments, recruited and trained volunteer clinic escorts and legal observers.


Our team is now preparing to return to New Orleans, and together with local supporters, volunteers, escorts and legal observers, *will work to keep the clinics safe and open [ https://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=11227 ]* [ https://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=11227 ] during a week-long Operation Rescue/Operation Save America July event attracting anti-abortion extremists nationwide.

We will make sure the clinic stays open when anti-abortion extremists gather for the trial.
