* I’m not sure where these photographs come from or who’s running the site, but at Internet K-Hole (what does that even mean?!) there is a limitless supply of found photos from the 70s and 80s . Each photo is a snapshot of an indescribable event often involving alcohol or nudity, with the best photos involving both. [2 of 3]

* “The Best Show” was a music and comedy radio show put out by New York’s WFMU for over fifteen years. There are literally thousands of hours available for download , and all of them for free. Along with sidekick Jon Wurster, host Tom Scharpling produced comedy similar to a modern update of the classic comedy team Bob and Ray. Surreal and brilliant, and always surprising. [3 of 3]
