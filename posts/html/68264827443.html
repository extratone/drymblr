You'll never look at your hamburger the same way

Dear Dry,

With Thanksgiving quickly approaching, families across the country are looking forward to rich and filling holiday feasts. While others celebrate though, fast-food workers-many of whom are expected to scrap by on just $7.25/hour-may struggle to afford such festivities.

Our new edition of Ms. [ http://store.feminist.org/getandgivems.aspx ] features today’s Rosie the Riveter: the woman taking your fast-food order. Fed up, she’s striking for a $15/hour paycheck that will allow her to cover basic needs and stop her from making impossible choices about food and housing.

Trust me: When you read the facts about a $200 billion industry that primarily employs - and exploits - women, you’ll never look at your hamburger the same way.
