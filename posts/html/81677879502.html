Today FFL President Serrin M. Foster celebrates
20 Years of leading Feminists For Life!
The FFL Board of Directors salutes Serrin, creator of the Women Deserve BetterÂ®
Than Abortion Campaign, by paying tribute to her signature efforts:
Twenty years of leading a renaissance of the original American feminism.
Twenty years of working tirelessly for a better world in which:

* No woman would be driven by desperation to abortion.
* Pregnancy, motherhood, and birthmotherhood are supported and celebrated.
* Campuses and workplaces support mothers in practical ways and do not force them
to choose between their education or career plans and their children.
* Society supports the role of mothers, values the role of fathers, and helps parents
provide both financial and emotional support for their children.
* Parents are respected, whether they decide to stay at home or return to work.
Twenty years of living out the spirit of Susan B. Anthony’s words:
“Sweeter even than to have had the joy of caring for children of my own has it been
to me to help bring about a better state of things for mothers generally, so that
their unborn little ones could not be willed away from them.”
Today, on her 20th Anniversary,
the FFL Board of Directors kicks off a
20 week campaign to honor Serrin’s
remarkable 20 years of leadership:
Saluting Serrin! 20 Years, 20 Milestones!
Each week, FFL will highlight one of twenty milestones achieved by FFL under Serrin’s
twenty years of leadership as FFL President.
The FFL Board takes the lead in paying tribute to Serrin with our special gift to
FFL of $5,000 in her honor.
The Board of Directors invites you to join the celebration with your tax-deductible
gift to Feminists For Life of America in honor of Serrin’s 20 years of leadership!
