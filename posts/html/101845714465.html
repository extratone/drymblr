Mickey Ashmore is the creator of Sabah , a niche brand of handmade, high-quality leather slip-on shoes for men and women. Inspired by his travels and time living in Istanbul, Mickey sought to revive the traditional Turkish slipper for today’s international traveler while maintaining the integrity of materials and craftsmanship. Mickey lives in the East Village and frequently travels to Gaziantep, Turkey, to meet with his craftsmen. INSTAGRAM
