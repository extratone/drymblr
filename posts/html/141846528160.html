Attending SEWSA, CLPP or the Toni Cade at Spelman? So Are We!

Dear Dry,


Ahh, springtime! The birds are chirping, the flowers are blooming and the conference circuit is heating up! We are ready to transition in like a lion and out like a lamb to warmer days, longer nights and a sweet springtime filled with feminist activism. Our Feminist Campus Organizers are hitting the conference tour and we want to meet up with your angelic feminist faces!


Our HBCU and Southern Campus Organizer Chelsea Yarborough and our Midwest Campus Organizer Mari Vangen are headed down South for two (yeah, you read that right - TWO!) conferences in South Carolina and Georgia! The first stop on the trip is to the Southeastern Women’s Studies Association Conference [ http://www.sewsa.net/2016-winthrop/ ] at Winthrop University in Rock Hill, S.C on March 31st. From South Carolina they’ll be headed to Spelman College in Atlanta, GA for the Toni Cade Bambara Conference, [ http://www.spelman.edu/academics/majors-and-programs/comparative-womens-studies/toni-cade-bambara-conference ] happening April 1st-2nd!


Catch our Mid Atlantic and Northeastern Campus Organizer Kelli Musick at the Civil Liberties and Public Policy Program’s [ https://clpp.hampshire.edu/conference ] annual conference at Hampshire College in Amhurst, MA, happening April 8th-10th! She’ll be tabling in the exhibitor’s hall with feminist goodies, ways you can get active on your campus, and her trademark fiery red mane, of course.


Drop us a line at campusteam@feminist.org if you’ll be in the area for these conferences - we’d love to see you!


In solidarity,


Your Feminist Campus Organizers

Chelsea, Edwith, Mari, and Kelli
