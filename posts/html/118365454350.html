Honor Your Mother...

I am so grateful that I can celebrate Mother’s Day with my mother this year. Yet,
while I honor her, I also recognize, that because of you, every day is Mother’s
Day.

A number of you have asked how you might honor your mother or those who were like
a mother to you. May I suggest a tribute gift which includes a gift membership (and
Welcome Kit) to new members ($35 minimum)?

You might also be inspired to sponsor five copies of “Raising Kids on a Shoestring”
and have us send it to a Pregnancy Resource Center, pastoral or campus counselors.
Let us know where you wish them to go, and we will let them know of your thoughtfulness.

This Saturday is also Birthmother’s Day.
