We want to send a HUGE thank you to everyone who seized the moment,
contacted their representatives, and ROSE for VAWA! The inclusive,
bi-partisan Senate version of the Violence Against Women Act passed
today and it would not have been possible without YOUR support,
outrage, emails, flash mobs, tweets, posts, events, telephone calls,
leadership, and action!

Our heartfelt thanks to all the women’s leaders and groups who worked
to make this happen. Special shout out to our One Billion Rising/NOW
coordinator Pat Reuss!
