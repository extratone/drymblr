RISE for Peace and an Immediate Ceasefire in Gaza

We - ONE BILLION RISING - a world movement of women and men working to
end violence against women and girls, and all the intersectional
injustices that sustain the impunity that keep it in place - call for
an immediate ceasefire. We call for an end to the occupation of
Palestine and all forms of oppression that lead to more violence.

http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=0G4Rw3RcI2N55NFa2Pq9dw

Already countless civilians have died - many of whom are women and
children.

We cry with and rise for the mothers who have lost their children and
families and who live in daily terror in this ongoing war.

We cry with those that have been occupied and diminished and who are
seeking recourse.

We know that our sisters on both sides long for connection, justice
and peace.

And we stand in solidarity with the women and men of Palestine and
peace loving people of Israel who demand an end to the bombings and
air strikes now.

250 Afghan women and girls. The center also provides, psychosocial,
and counseling support.
