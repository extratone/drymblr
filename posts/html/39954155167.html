Dear Dry ,
We are planning tours now for the Spring (see below). Because of the generosity
of some donors, we are able to offer reduced rates so that you can host the best
pro-life feminist speakers in the nation.

I know many of you are very concerned about the issue of rape and pregnancy. Consider
inviting Joyce McCauley-Benner [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001cy6IdJu4wJt3MpPhYlGfnGToSk-zS48ZyufRgx3L9YLTjw5cCTZgTtoVmcero3M7MkQmB1tGoFJA0YhSYmfA6Uh2eK-8gAJqqzFpbwIKjGCau5vaMXrpL5g8QfiKe-6punS1sU-y_LN_9k_B1egv7X0ry6gYfnRRzdNFjLtlfWs=]
to campus. She didn’t know who the father was, but she knew who the mother was and
chose victory over violence!

Our NEW speaker is Jewels Green. Jewels discovered she was pregnant at 17 and was
pressured into having an abortion that led to depression and a suicide attempt.
She then worked in an abortion center much of her young adulthood and became pro-life
years later. She offers insider information from behind the closed doors of the
abortion industry and unique insight into the highly personal journey of conversion.
FFL was the first pro-life organization Jewels joined and worked as a volunteer
before coming on board as the Editor of FFL’s magazine, The American Feminist.

And don’t forget, if you need ads, they are FREE and downloadable [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001cy6IdJu4wJvBImD3BZtXvF0IvB3gRoQPQPgFdIWG48qzKToF2hmI3sDf5jjBVz84jjDSNK8Z_epb0ZqRGQHMlGoZWGPgaG8AIGYYWpdO56SnSKvFnAy8tAuHQdLpi2WT5pA6PAikO-x6dw-D56AXfw==]!
If you haven’t rec’d your Revolution on Campus kit to grow your group, order yours
FREE [mailto:coordinator@ffloncampus.org] from me now. FFL is also here with the
original feminist historical research to share with those who are not yet aware
of our feminist foremothers’ stand against abortion.

As the pioneers of pregnant and parenting resources on campus, contact me for help.
Don’t forget to take FFL’s Pregnancy Resource Survey [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001cy6IdJu4wJubocf80QZkVsPnQ9E6Mae-Ztz4JAyq-RNxlGaKkrBETNEbgWD22RVdOVx8Q8NbE0UEhBrieJNI4ffq4dVtjMYoeiE8glHBP-3_csdHdtCd44HLVtU3nWHt]TM
