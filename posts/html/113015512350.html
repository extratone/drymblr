RISE FOR THE RAISE in NYC and beyond!

Restaurant Opportunities Centers United (ROC United) and the One Fair
Wage Campaign kicks-off Women’s history month with a historic
victory. Last week, Governor Cuomo announced a raise to $7.50 an hour
for New York’s 400,000 tipped workers.

Dedicated activism and support for the ongoing campaign has made this
victory possible, but there is still more to do in order to review the
entire two-tiered wage system at a national level.
