Nine hundred attendees heard FFL President Serrin Foster present “The Feminist Case
Against Abortion” at the Washington State “Cornerstone” Catholic Conference–and
our booth was literally mobbed after Serrin spoke. People are hungry for our rich
pro-life feminist history and message of hope and focus on resources and support!

Here’s what the organizer, Pam Gunderson, had to say:

“Serrin had a compelling presence and a lot of credibility. The history and context
she provided were unique and eye-opening. Even long-time pro-lifers in the audience
were surprised at how much of this background they did not know; and those who are
more comfortable with feminism than with pro-life activity were captured.”

In advance of the conference, the Northwest Catholic featured an article entitled
“Women Deserve Better than Abortion” by Serrin, the creator of the “Women Deserve
Better than Abortion” campaign.

Read for yourself and please forward this essential article onto family and friends–those
who agree and those you’d like to invite to our movement.
