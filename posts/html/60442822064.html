Ready for feminists to take on Washington?

Dear Dry,

I’m excited to announce some of this year’s Women, Money, Power Summit honorees: Rep. Rosa DeLauro (D - Conn.), Texas state Sen. Leticia Van de Putte, and Barbara Arnwine, executive director of the Lawyer’s Committee for Civil Rights Under Law.

The Summit is bringing together a great combination of feminist leaders, thinkers, speakers and activists, as well as top politicians. On Oct. 8, we’ll have a series of panels dedicated to fighting the War on Women and an amazing luncheon. On Oct. 9, we’ll head to Capital Hill for a Congressional lobby day.

We also already have 32 co-sponsoring organizations! Trust me, you won’t want to miss miss it. [ http://feministmajority.org/women-money-power/2013WomenMoneyPower/registration.htm ]

Congresswoman DeLauro has been a longtime champion of women’s rights, pushing for equal pay and paid sick days for all. Van de Putte made national headlines in June when she stood up for women’s reproductive rights in Texas. Arnwine is internationally renowned for contributions to civil rights and is leading the fight against voter suppression laws.

At a time when extremists are attempting to roll back both women’s rights and civil rights, we’re excited to honor three champions for social justice.

Dry, this is an event you will not want to miss! Grab one of the tickets before they run out. [ http://feministmajority.org/women-money-power/2013WomenMoneyPower/registration.htm ]
