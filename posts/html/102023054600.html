Food Chains Premieres in Select Theaters 21 November

http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=jMVHRu9mUzL2tHhpB2kBBQ

Food Chains, a new doc which showcases the economic injustice and
abuse rampant in farm labor, is coming out in select theaters in the
U.S on 21 November.

Executive produced by Eva Longoria, Eric Schlosser and Abigail Disney,
narrated by Forest Whitaker, and featuring global activists including
Eve, Food Chains also reveals the forces behind that exploitation
through the narrative of the Coalition of Immokalee Workers (CIW), an
intrepid group of tomato pickers in Florida who are battling the 5
trillion dollar global supermarket industry - and winning. The film
premiered at the 2014 Berlin Film Festival and screened subsequently
at the Tribeca Film Festival and Guadalajara Film Festival.
