As we celebrate Roe v. Wade’s 40th Anniversary, we are painfully aware of the increasing threats to reproductive rights. We must keep our nation’s clinics safe and open.

Support work to defend clinics in honor of Roe’s anniversary today. [ https://salsa.democracyinaction.org/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=9608 ]

The Feminist Majority Foundation’s National Clinic Access Project, the oldest and largest in the country, has worked with besieged clinics for 25 years to counter the constant attacks and threats. *Right now, FMF is sending organizers to work with clinic health care workers in Wichita, Kansas, and in Jackson, Mississippi.*

In Wichita, where Dr. George Tiller was brutally murdered almost four years ago, a group of courageous medical professionals are working to re-open Dr. Tiller’s clinic. Our law enforcement experts and organizers are traveling to Wichita to provide critically needed assistance. We must make sure that what happened to Dr. Tiller never happens again.

The only abortion provider in Mississippi, the Jackson Women’s Health Organization – long the target of extremists – has never stopped providing its critical services. Operation Save America/Operation Rescue is traveling from North Carolina to Mississippi to target the clinic from January 19th-22nd. We are sending FMF campus organizers to assist with community organizing and clinic defense. What’s more, the state has passed a TRAP law in an effort to close this clinic.

Please help the Feminist Majority Foundation do organizing and security work to help these embattled healthcare workers and doctors.
