 Eve’s Newest Play In The Body of The World World Premieres
at American Repertory Theater (ART) in May 

art-inthebody.jpg

Eve will be world premiering her newest work In The Body of The
World - at American Repertory Theater (ART) in Cambridge, MA from
May 10-29, 2016. Tickets are going fast, reserve your seat today.
http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=sLEd14sw-DrYABgM7I16wA

Written & performed by Eve and directed by Tony award winning
director Diane Paulus. In this world premiere adaptation of her
critically acclaimed 2013 memoir, Eve celebrates the strength and joy
that connect a single body to the planet. As an activist and artist,
she has spent her career speaking about the female body. While working
in the Congo, where war continues to inflict devastating violence on
women, she was diagnosed with stage III/IV uterine cancer. This
diagnosis erased the boundaries between her work and her own body. In
this raw, humorous, and bold performance, Eve charts the connections
between the personal and the public, inviting and challenging all of
us to come back into our bodies, and thus the world.

On 22 and 25 May, City of Joy’s Christine Schuler Deschryver (V-Day’s
Congo Director and the Director of City of Joy) will be doing
talkbacks after the performance. Don’t miss the rare and incredible
chance to come together with other supporters and hear the amazing
update about City of Joy and V-World Farm firsthand from Christine.
