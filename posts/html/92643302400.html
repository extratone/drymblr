Dear Dry,

This past weekend Operation Rescue/Save America started its week-long siege of clinics in New Orleans, Louisiana. I’m writing to you from New Orleans where our Clinic Defense team is working with local officials, community leaders, and volunteer escorts to ensure protection for the clinics and doctors.

*We need your help to keep the city’s two clinics open and its doctors, patients, and staff safe. * [ http://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=11227 ]

Over the weekend, anti-abortion extremists distributed “WANTED”-style flyers featuring photos of doctors, their home addresses, and clinic information at one of the doctors’ homes. We were there to coordinate clinic defenders and monitor and record the disruptive protests.

Similar “WANTED”-style posters appeared in the early 1990s before doctors were murdered by extremists in Florida.

But OR/OSA’s efforts to intimidate and threaten the doctor backfired. Outraged, the doctor’s neighbors came out of their homes to join our clinic defenders and protest the extremists’ tactics. One quickly made and posted a big sign in their front yard that read:

“WE SUPPORT FREEDOM! AND WE LOVE OUR NEIGHBOR.”

So many people in New Orleans have stepped forward to lend their support and time. They are so gracious to us, and we are working so fabulously together.

Our work to defend abortion clinics has become even more difficult in the wake of the recent Supreme Court decision striking down clinic buffer zones. *That’s why we are asking for your support.* [ http://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=11227 ]

After New Orleans, our team will travel to Jackson, Mississippi, for the trial of anti-abortion extremist and OR/OSA leader Chet Gallagher. Gallagher was arrested earlier at the Jackson Women’s Health Organization, the only clinic that remains open in Mississippi. With your support, we will be there to make sure the clinic stays open when anti-abortion extremists gather for the trial.

*Please make a tax-deductible contribution today to support the National Clinic Access Project and our work to defend clinics.* [ http://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=11227 ]

For Women’s Lives,
duVergne Gaines
NCAP Director
