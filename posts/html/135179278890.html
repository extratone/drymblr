Why Gloria Steinem started Ms.

Dry,

Gloria Steinem co-founded Ms. magazine 43 years ago when other magazines just weren’t interested in women’s issues. As Gloria recently told NPR’s Fresh Air:

“"It was sort of, well, if [another magazine had] an article saying women are equal, then in order to be objective, [it would] have to publish one saying they’re not.” “

When Ms. magazine launched, the pundits were predicting the magazine would run out of things to say in 6 issues. Well, 43 years later, our reporting and truth-telling still fills the pages of what’s been called one of the most influential magazines in U.S. history. And we need your help to make sure Ms. stays strong and independent.
