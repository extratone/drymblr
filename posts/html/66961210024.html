The modern-day Rosie the Riveter

Dear Dry,

$200 billion dollars. That’s how much money is grossed annually by the fast-food industry off the backs of workers who make as little as $7.25/hour.

But fed-up fast-food workers are now striking nationwide for higher pay, demanding a $15/hour minimum wage.

Our new edition of Ms. [ http://store.feminist.org/getandgivems.aspx ] features today’s Rosie the Riveter, the woman taking your fast-food order and serving up meals “with a smile, ” even though she can barely make ends meets for her family at home.

Trust me: When you read the facts about an industry that primarily employs - and exploits - women, you’ll never look at your hamburger the same way.
