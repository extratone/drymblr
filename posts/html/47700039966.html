BRUSSELS RISING
by Colette de Troy & Valentina Brogna, Regional Coordinators
(European Women’s Lobby)
One Billion Rising was an exciting way of campaigning against violence
against women. We used music and art, we danced and sang, using our
bodies to protest and to occupy public spaces so that our message
could not be ignored and could be conveyed in a positive, non-violent
way.
