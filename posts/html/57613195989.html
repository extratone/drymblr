Dear Dry,

Wendy Davis took a stand (literally!) for reproductive justice in Texas and inspired men and women nationwide to fight outrageous attempts to close women’s health clinics. “Ms”. is proud to honor Sen. Davis’ historic stand on our Summer cover.

Get the exciting and special new issue of Ms [ http://store.feminist.org/getandgivems.aspx ]. for all the exclusive updates on the state laws threatening to close women’s health clinics in Texas, Virginia, North Carolina, Ohio, and 10 other states, as well as on-the-ground reports of what advocates are doing to fight back.
