VSL // An Album Inspiration

* Maine: I no longer live in my home state of Maine , but I reminisced about it (and the concept of home) on my new album, AFTER. The final song on the album, ‘Atlas,’ showcases my love for Maine with lines like “funny how a good thing opens your eyes, makes you feel the branches, leads you to the pines.” [1 of 3]

* Incorruptibility: There has been a story on my dad’s Italian side of the family of a great-great aunt whose body was exhumed years after burial and found intact. After a second exhumation, her body was deemed to be incorruptible and she was canonized and reburied in The Vatican. I felt compelled to sing about it on ‘Billions of Eyes!’ [2 of 3]

* The Great Pyramids: Both 'Heretic’ and 'Spat Out Spit’ have moments inspired by reading a theory that the great pyramids were not burial tombs, and that they are aligned with the stars of Orion’s belt . [3 of 3]
