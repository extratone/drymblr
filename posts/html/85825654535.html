Finding a good gym in the city can be harder than finding an apartment…without a broker’s fee…that has laundry in the building.

In other words, (almost) impossible.

And while landlords don’t offer one-day trials, we are a club that will (because commitment is hard)!

Reebok Sports Club NY is hooking up VSL readers with a complimentary, one-day membership so you can test drive this sweat temple for free.

Reebok Sports Club NY offers up to 170 classes per week—from yoga to group resistance training to its one-of-a-kind (and fun!) Recess class.

Get in shape using space-age(ish) machinery, swimming laps in the 25-yard indoor pool, playing ball on two regulation-size basketball courts and punching out frustrations in the boxing studio.

If you like what you see and smell—seriously, we have MALIN+GOETZ in our locker rooms—you can extend your membership for free perks.

With a membership, you’ll score a complimentary wellness package (valued at about $400)…not to mention a healthier lifestyle.
