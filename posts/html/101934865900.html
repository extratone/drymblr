Purveyor of French macarons in fun American flavors, Dana’s Bakery is transforming classic holiday desserts into delicious macarons. November features a “Pie Box” of Pecan Pie, Berry Cobbler, and Sweet Potato Pie macarons It’s available all month, and you can pre-order now for Thanksgiving to beat the holiday rush! For December we’re offering a Holiday Box with Egg Nog, Sugar Cookie, and Chocolate Hazel“nut-cracker” macarons, as well as a Hanukkah Box with a pairing of golden Chocolate Gelt and Jelly Doughnut flavors!

We also have some great new products this year, including gift cards that can be purchased online and used on the website available in $25, $50, and $100 amounts. We also have beautiful, deluxe Gift Boxes that come with our standard flavor assortment, or you can choose between themed Chocolate Lover’s and Party Pack assortments. The Mac Daddy Tower and its counterpart, the Macaron Mini Tower , also make perfect host/hostesses gifts! The Mini Tower comes with 40 macarons in a variety of flavors and is presented in a 4 tier tower-style display tray. It’s a great way to make an impression at any holiday party.

The best-selling Macaron of the Month Club is still our favorite offering. Subscriptions are available in 3, 6, and 12 month packages… it really is the gift that keeps on giving!

We’re also still taking special orders , corporate gifting, and events requests through the holiday season. All of our macarons are made with almond flour, gluten free, and certified Kosher by Kof-K. And of course, we have local delivery in NYC, and ship nationwide!
