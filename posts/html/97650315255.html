The Violence Against Women Act is now twenty years old!! Feminists for Life was
the only pro-life group active in the women’s coalition to support the original
legislation. We celebrate the many lives of women and children who were saved from
domestic violence and the women who are truly survivors of sexual assault. Our
heartfelt thanks to our members and supporters who made this possible. Read insightful
articles and find resources here: http://www.feministsforlife.org/vawa/ [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?f=001daR8FhUla35u7OGBZsDUDsZ6-NVqrihD2LkEY3HpgGmg-q2gXR7Mywjc994En9mhxbQu9atrE_ocgTrSTf1fWzCKkkgbazW0D5KGjQlSFi4N1EWGMH1oX_OAmbfsT6wb0jGta45c3J1aqqTFT1w6BleosHR_DTrU35xFc7jk_ZHsqBp-9EM_CTEj1wwUIPTq&c=goOaW3QgzudavILwwr0IQE4Y9G_rmyVKL00Wo-etzfPR-2_NgDbg_g==&ch=iPRmR68-uW73FDaT50siXh6VdOfehUovjUC_7H4DyIj6MqA2RNep4w==]

of the largest in the country!) and the University of Wisconsin at Platteville.

California during the Roe v. Wade anniversary events.

Joyce McCauley Benner will be at Stanford and return to Berkeley. (Last spring
600 students at Berkeley heard her. They want her back!)
