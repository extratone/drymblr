Happy National Voter Registration Day! [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/09/24/im-a-registered-virginia-feminist-celebrating-national-voter-registration-day-by-taking-action/ ]

It’s National Voter Registration Day [ http://www.nationalvoterregistrationday.org/ ], and it couldn’t possibly matter more. In the past year, we’ve seen unprecendented attacks on women’s health and equality; voting can be a key part of fighting back [ http://feministcampus.org/vote/ ]. (In some states, it can also be an act of protest against restrictive student voting laws [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/08/28/roundtable-students-speak-out-against-voting-restrictions-targeting-young-people/ ] that make it hard for us to be heard.) Kristy took to our blog today to write about Virginia’s upcoming election, and why being a registered feminist matters [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/09/24/im-a-registered-virginia-feminist-celebrating-national-voter-registration-day-by-taking-action/ ]. Make sure you get registered today [ http://www.rockthevote.com/rtv_voter_registration.html ]!

Mapping A Movement: Ohio [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/tag/mapping-a-movement/ ]

Ever wondered what it’s like to be a feminist organizer? Our campus team has the amazing opportunity to see all the action as well as the behind-the-scenes strategies taking place on campuses nationwide - and now they’re telling their stories. In our new series, Mapping A Movement [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/tag/mapping-a-movement/ ], organizers will share their trip diaries - talking about everything from moldy airport croissants to statewide movements and local recruitment. Check it out: In our first-ever installment, Maddie and Edwith take Ohio by storm [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/09/16/mapping-a-movement-maddie-and-edwith-hit-the-road-in-ohio/ ].

Stand With Ohio Women! [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/09/23/we-wont-go-back-stand-with-ohio-women-october-2/ ]

Ohio women are mad as heck, and they’re not gonna take it anymore. You can stand with them at the Ohio State House in Columbus on October 2 for the We Won’t Go Back Rally [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/09/23/we-wont-go-back-stand-with-ohio-women-october-2/ ], in which femininsts from across the state will make some noise to urge lawmakers to stop attacking reproductive rights! Maddie and Carmen will be there on the ground to make some noise, and various campus groups from around the area will be bringing out their folks to raise a storm. Don’t miss it!

Founding Feminists [ http://feminist.org/blog/index.php/category/founding-feminists/ ]

The days of yore belong to us, too! Are you tired of hearing the same old same old about women’s history? Do you sometimes wonder what Alice Paul was up to when she was 83? Are you curious about the suffrage movement in New Zealand? Did you miss Billie Jean King’s amazing and groundbreaking game, set, match? FMF’s got you covered with their new daily herstory column, Founding Feminists [ http://feminist.org/blog/index.php/category/founding-feminists/ ].
