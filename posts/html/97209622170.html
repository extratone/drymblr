RISE w/ Eve & OBR at The People’s Climate March on Sunday, 21
September, in NYC!

Dear Risers,

We are in the midst of an environmental crisis and the time has come
to unite with our sisters and brothers across all sectors to demand a
new model of sustainable development, a model that includes safe,
people-centered renewable energy systems and reverses our current path
of environmental destruction.

On Sunday, 21 September, I am RISING at the People’s Climate March in
NYC and I hope you’ll join me!

The March will take place two days before the United Nations Climate
Summit, and we need to show the world leaders that we will not stand
by while they continue to ignore this crisis.

Let’s help create the Largest Climate March in History!

We’ve seen the power of One Billion Rising to create a global shift in
consciousness around violence against women and girls. Let’s use this
power to do the same with violence against our mother Earth.

We cannot ignore the historical and interrelated destructive treatment
of both women and nature in our current dominator model of societies.
The longstanding history of violating the Earth and violating women is
deeply rooted in the same problem and societal imbalances. To achieve
long term solutions, we will need to deal with root causes and
systemic change and this will mean liberating the silenced and
oppressed voices of women and nature.

Together we stand for the Earth and future generations. Together we
can make our voices heard. Together we can create a revolution.

Details for the meeting point and route below.

With gratitude and love,

Eve
