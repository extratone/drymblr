Get ready for some fantastic speakers

Dear Dry,

Please join us at the Women, Money, Power Summit on October 8 at the Capital Hilton Hotel in Washington, D.C.!

I’m very excited that we are bringing together such a great combination of feminist activists, experts and readers. You don’t want to miss it, register today! [ http://feministmajority.org/women-money-power/2013WomenMoneyPower/registration.htm ]

The Summit is being kicked off Tuesday at 9:30 a.m. with a lively discussion on how to stop the War on Women and its attacks on everything from women’s health to voting rights, food stamps and our wallets. The second panel of the morning will focus on some cutting-edge research on women’s global reproductive rights.

At our luncheon at noon, we’ll honor trailblazing feminists: Rep. Rosa DeLauro (D - Conn.), Texas state Sen. Leticia Van de Putte, and Barbara Arnwine, executive director of the Lawyer’s Committee for Civil Rights Under Law. And that only gets us to 2 p.m.!

Our first afternoon summit discussion will present to attendees key strategies on ratifying the international women’s treaty CEDAW, passing the Equal Rights Amendment, organizing on critical upcoming ballot initiatives and ending violence against women.

We’ll wrap up with a town hall discussion on the Affordable Care Act, emphasizing what women need to know and how advocates can maximize its success.

Dry, I’m sure you can see what a fantastic event this will be. 38 organizations think so too and have already signed up to be sponsors! [ http://feministmajority.org/women-money-power/2013WomenMoneyPower/registration.htm ]

Visit the Women, Money, Power website [ http://feministmajority.org/women-money-power/2013WomenMoneyPower/registration.htm ] for more information. And stay tuned for upcoming updates
