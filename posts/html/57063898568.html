Calling all food lovers seeking a new culinary milestone: Make memories at a pop-up dinner hosted by the Patrón Secret Dining Society. Each location, chef, menu and evening is kept secret until the last minute. Mystery tastes good. Previous dinners featured delicious treats from the likes of Rick Bayless and Michelle Bernstein. Venues ranged from Patrón Founder’s personal ranch in Texas to the Spanish Monastery in Miami. In short, you’ll eat in style.
