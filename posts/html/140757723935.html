Dear Dry,

Can’t make it to want to connected to all the fun? Are you already getting the dreaded FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) feels? Have you already finished your Netflix queue and are you DYING for something to satisfy your need for incredible feminist programming?

Fret not, dear one. I come bearing a wonderful gift-*We’ll be livestreaming the plenary sessions and town hall of the National Young Feminist Leadership Conference for your home viewing pleasure!* Join in all the awe-inspiring, magnificent feminist joy right from your couch! It’ll be just like you’re here with us in D.C., except you’ll get to stay cozy in your home. The best of both worlds-combined!

We’ll be sending the livestream link out on the day of the conference and we will also post it on our social media (so find us on the interwebs if you haven’t already [ https://www.facebook.com/feministcampus/?fref=ts ]!).

*And make sure you use the hashtagees at the conference and other folks viewing from home!*

In solidarity,

Chelsea Yarborough-HBCU and Southern National Campus Organizer
