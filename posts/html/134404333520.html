Dear Dry,

We know you’ve been waiting, and we’re thrilled to announce the *12th Annual National Young Feminist Leadership Conference [ http://feministcampus.org/conferences/2016-nyflc/ ]* will be taking place from March 12-14, 2016, in the Washington, D.C. area.

We’ll be covering topics ranging from global women’s rights, to reproductive justice, to campus organizing tips. Our workshops will feature campus activists, political leaders, and other feminist public figures. You’ll have the chance to take action, get inspired, network with other activists, grab some feminist gear, or explore the nation’s capital. Last year was great and this year will be even better!

We want you – and your whole feminist campus group! – to attend, so start your planning now to receive funding from your college or university to help you get there! Stay posted by checking this portal, signing up for our Campus Alerts [ http://feministcampus.org/sign-up/?table=supporter&organization_KEY=1400&redirect=http%3A%2F%2Ffeministcampus.org%2Fsign-up%2F&Email= ], and following us on Twitter @feministcampus [ https://twitter.com/feministcampus ]. Use the hashtagnect with other attendees and tell us how excited you are! Questions? Don’t hesitate to contact us at nyflc@feminist.org or call 703-522-2214 and ask to speak to a conference representative
