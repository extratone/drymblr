*Join us as we call on President Obama* [ https://www.thunderclap.it/en/projects/19746-us-abortion-policy-hurts-women ] to take executive action to ensure that women and girls all over the world have access to life-saving abortion care.

Each year, an estimated 47,000 women will die from unsafe abortion. Nearly 800 women around the world die in pregnancy and childbirth every day – 287,000 women a year, mostly in the developing world – and millions more suffer from injury, infection, or disease during pregnancy and childbirth.

*Tell President Obama to act now to help save lives and alleviate the suffering of women worldwide* [ https://www.thunderclap.it/en/projects/19746-us-abortion-policy-hurts-women ]. For over 40 years, the Helms Amendment to U.S. foreign assistance has been misapplied to block access to safe abortion care for women – even in cases of rape, incest, and life endangerment. President Obama can correct that.

The Helms Amendment prevents U.S. foreign assistance for abortion “as a method of family planning” - but it “allows” U.S. funds to help women who are survivors of rape or incest, and for those with life endangering pregnancies, to access safe abortion care.

*We are joining with many other organizations* [ https://www.thunderclap.it/en/projects/19746-us-abortion-policy-hurts-women ] to call on President Obama to stand with women and girls in the developing world.
