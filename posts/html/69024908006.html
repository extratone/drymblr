Dear Dry,
Feminists for Life of America’s Board of Directors invites active members and donors
to a private briefing by teleconference on Thursday, December 12, 2013 at 8 PM ET.
FFL President Serrin M. Foster will report to the membership about the impact of
our work, especially on college campuses, and new efforts to reach legislators.
You’ll also have an opportunity to hear from one of our many impressive interns,
Katherine Beck, about what she accomplished–and how we intend to build on this
effort in 2014!

Active members and donors should RSVP as soon as possible [mailto:RSVP@feministsforlife.org]as
space is truly limited. Teleconference instructions for the private briefing will
be emailed back to you on Wednesday night.


No need to dress up, shovel show, go out in a cold rain, spend money on transportation,
or pay a babysitter. Just curl up in a comfy chair and have an opportunity to hear
how your sacrifice made a tangible difference.

Our mission is to systematically eliminate the reasons that drive women to abortion–primarily
a lack of resources and support. Women deserve better than abortion. And every child
deserves life.


We don’t succeed without supporters like you.
