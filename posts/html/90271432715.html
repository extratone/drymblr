All employees in our skill based organization work in all areas including advocacy,
public education especially our flagship College Outreach Program, online efforts
and various publications–media, marketing, and resource development.

One position emphasizes strong tech skills, member services and administration.
Basic computer skills as well as knowledge of the Internet, social media, E-Tapestry
, InDesign, Photoshop, and Microsoft Office programs are essential.

The other job requires a strong editor, writer, and volunteer coordinator to move
our mission and goals forward.Knowledge of our pro-life feminist history would be
helpful, especially for the writer, editor and volunteer coordinator. This person
would also have strong interpersonal skills.

Qualified candidates are college educated, committed to FFL’s pro-life feminist
mission.

Applicants who will be considered for both positions must be mission and goal driven,
detail oriented, able to manage time well and hit deadlines, well organized, able
to work independently and as part of a team while working on various projects simultaneously.

Candidates for employment who have the above mentioned skills are invited to send
cover letter and resume demonstrating their commitment to our pro-life feminist
mission and work experience demonstrating their skills in respective areas.

Please identify if you are applying for the tech position or editor, writer, and
volunteer program coordinator in your cover letter to resume@feministsforlife.org
[mailto:resume@feministsforlife.org]. Please include your resume in the email. No
attachments please.

Comprehensive health care is available. Immediate openings.
