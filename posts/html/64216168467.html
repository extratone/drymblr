Today is the 15th annual Love Your Body Day [ http://loveyourbody.nowfoundation.org/ ]! The Campus Team commemorated the occasion on our blog [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/10/15/roundtable-happy-love-your-body-day/ ] by exploring what “body love” really means to them, as well as reflecting on their own struggles with body images and journeys to body empowerment.

Loving our bodies is a political revolution; every day, women face messages that their bodies are “inadequate,” “imperfect,” or need alteration or fixing.

Our deepest hope is that you spend this day reflecting on just how awesome you (and your body!) are, and in that spirit we’re inviting you to share your stories on our LYBD blog post and roundtable [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/index.php/2013/10/15/roundtable-happy-love-your-body-day/ ] and download our LYBD postcard [ http://feministcampus.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/love-yr-body-card.pdf ] to share the love. (You can also join today’s conversation on Twitter using the tag [ http://twitter.com/search?q=%23LYBD&src=typd ]!)
