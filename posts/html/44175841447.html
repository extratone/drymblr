responsibilities, and how college activists can transform their campus using
our model “tools for schools.”

right? A fundamental freedom? Or a reflection that we have not met the needs
of women?”
* We explain our pro-life feminist philosophy, identify who is at highest
risk of abortion, our record of achievement and what we are doing to move forward
with woman-centered solutions.
