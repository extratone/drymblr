An Evening with Dr. Denis Mukwege & Eve Ensler is part of the
RISE4JUSTICE Speakers Series which will examine the meaning of justice
to activists working globally to end gender violence and oppression,
while looking at impunity caused by the intersection of poverty,
racism, war, theplunder of the environment, capitalism, imperialism,
and patriarchy. The series is presented by V-Day, the global activist
movement to end violence againstwomen and girls, and ABC Carpet &
Home.

*100% of the proceeds will go to the Panzi Foundation, supporting the
ongoing work of Panzi Hospital in Bukavu, DRC and creating new
outreach projects to rural clinics and communities.
