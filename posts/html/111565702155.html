* Mark Urio is a favorite decorative painter of ours and we are still obsessed with the faux-agate walls he executed for the Mish New York jewelry shop on Broome Street. It makes the real jewels pale in comparison. [1 of 3]

design 20 Feb 2015

Jesse Carrier and Mara Miller, the husband/wife design duo behind Carrier and Company Interiors Ltd , have created rooms for famous names like Anna Wintour, Jerry Seinfeld, and Annie Leibovitz. Recently, they designed residential spaces for Carnegie Park , a new luxury building on NYC’s Upper East Side.
