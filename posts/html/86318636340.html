Ms. in the Classroom presents:
Teaching Women’s Studies Online
Part of the Women’s Studies Summer Technology Institute
May 31, 2014 • UMD College Park • RSVP Now [ https://org.salsalabs.com/o/1400/p/salsa/event/common/public/?event_KEY=81980 ]

At this one-day intensive workshop, you will learn to:
* Adapt an existing course for online delivery and/or develop a new, “born digital” online course
* Integrate best practices in online course design and feminist pedagogy
* Explore digital tools for developing interactive, multi-media teaching environments
* Engage students with digital resources for the feminist classroom


Led by: Julie R. Enszer, Ph.D., Women’s Studies, University of Maryland College Park
and Janell Hobson, Ph.D., Women’s Studies, University at Albany, SUNY
and Agatha Beins, Ph.D., Women’s Studies, Texas Woman’s University
