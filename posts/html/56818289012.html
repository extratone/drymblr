Bait-and-Switch in North Carolina

Feminist Majority Foundation - Equality around the world


Dear Dry,

North Carolina Gov. Pat McCroy is about to pull a bait-and-switch.

This week, the Governor is expected to sign into law incredibly restrictive abortion legislation that would close all but one state clinic. Similar to the recently-passed Texas bill, the North Carolina legislation bans abortion at 20 weeks and imposes unneccessary regulations on abortion providers.

As if that wasn’t bad enough, Gov. McCroy said during his 2012 campaign that he wouldn’t impose any abortion restrictions if elected.

Take a look at this 30-second excerpt to see for yourself: [ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XJkGj_SbpbU ]

It’s unbelievable, isn’t it? Just nine months after making such a promise, he completely goes back on his words. He’s defending his action by saying the legislation doesn’t restrict abortion, it makes it safer. If closing all but one clinic isn’t restricting abortion, what is?
