RISE For Climate Justice On Monday, 29 September

GlobalWomensClimateJusticeDOAImage4[1].png

One Billion Rising is joining grassroots activists, Indigenous
leaders, business leaders, scientists, policy makers, farmers,
academics and culture-shapers in collaboration to RISE for Climate
Justice! Women’s Earth and Climate Action Network is
organizing the Global Women’s Climate Justice Day of Action for
Monday, 29 September.

http://action.vday.org/site/R?i=Lvaflyc-oKZObFZu3YgXUQ

Join us and women worldwide in organizing decentralized local actions
- from marches & sit-ins, to educational workshops, art
displays, and tree plantings. We are demonstrating the diversity and
strength of the women’s climate justice movement, and calling
for our voices are brought to the forefront of upcoming UN climate
negotiations.

PLAN your action on (or before) 29 September! Actions can range from
simply creating a sign and taking a photo displaying your climate
message, to putting up a solar panel, planting a tree, to
marches, sit-ins, educational events, art projects, etc.
