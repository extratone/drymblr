Sandhya Nankani is the founder of Literary Safari , an indie developer committed to minding the diversity gap in kids digital media . Literary Safari recently released Dentist Bird: A West African Folktale , the first in a series of global folktale-inspired game-rich book apps. TWITTER

* Up and Down is an interactive storybook app uses a split screen format to take kids through a day in the life of two very different children—one in the western Sahara desert and the other in the Western world—and demonstrates the commonalities that tie kids around the world together. [2 of 3]

* Amazon Prime is piloting a bunch of new children’s series. Our favorite is Sara Solves It , a math-focused detective show starring a brown brother-sister duo, set in what seems to be NYC, and featuring and a culturally-diverse cast of kids. (More about it here .) I love that culture isn’t the show’s primary focus, but rather it’s just about curious, smart kids who happen to be brown. [3 of 3]
